Henry A. Larsen's Contributions to Canada

How and why did Henry come to Canada?

Henry came to Canada by sea as an immigrant because he was inspired by Roald Amundsen after he explored the arctic.

What challenges did Henry face?

Back in the day, the world had way less technology. On ships, there were tons of struggles with communication and navigation. As well, ships were made without all the resources we can use today, which lead to them being stuck in ice many times while far out. Arctic exploration as well was not a high priority back then, so there was often a struggle to get the needed supplies and financial support which his missions. Another big issue would be cultural barries. Henry would've had to of adjusted to living how Indigenous people would and as well learn their languages. Overall, there were many struggles that had to be overcome.

What mark did Henry leave on Canada?

Henry's voyages thourhg the waters helped show that Canada could control it's Arctic waters better, showing what routes are easy to access. Henry as well strengthened the RCMP's northern prescence, he showed theres more places up north where the RCMP can control. Henry became a Superintendent of the RCMp and commanded the "G Division" of the RCMP which is in charge of Northern Ontario.
